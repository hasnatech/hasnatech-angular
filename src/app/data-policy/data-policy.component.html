Data Protection Policy

Introduction

At Hasna Technology, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our clients' and users' personal data. This Data Protection Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and safeguarding of personal information.

Scope

This policy applies to all personal data collected, processed, or stored by Hasna Technology, whether through our websites, applications, or any other business activities.

Principles

1. Data Minimization

We collect only the necessary personal data required for the purpose it was obtained. We do not process excessive or irrelevant information.

2. Consent

We obtain clear and unambiguous consent from individuals before collecting or processing their personal data.

3. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

4. Data Accuracy

We strive to maintain accurate and up-to-date personal data and promptly correct any inaccuracies.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data for the minimum period necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law.

6. Data Subject Rights

We respect the rights of data subjects, including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and data portability. Requests can be made via our Contact Information.

7. Data Sharing

We only share personal data with third parties when necessary for the fulfillment of our services, and we ensure that they also uphold data protection standards.

8. Data Breach Response

In the event of a data breach, we have procedures in place to notify affected individuals and authorities as required by law.

9. Data Protection Officer (DPO)

We have appointed a Data Protection Officer responsible for overseeing data protection compliance. Contact details are available at our Contact Information.

Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding our Data Protection Policy, please contact our Data Protection Officer

Review and Updates

This Data Protection Policy is subject to periodic review and updates. Any changes will be communicated through our website or directly to affected parties.
